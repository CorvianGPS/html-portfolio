The Best Movies According to Gabriel


My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shawshank Redemption

One of my favourite movies, had an interesting plot from the start all the way to the beginning.

Oppenheimer

How it manages to make a biopic distinction between the creator of the atomic bomb and just another genius human being is incredible.

Avengers Endgame

The amount of action and amazing characters on the screen was fantastic.
